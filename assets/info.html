Information

This app is a Demo application designed to explain the different life cycles of services.

The documentation of the Service class is quite clear but it's difficult to get a clear understanding of how things work in an app, how services life cycles and activities life cycles differ from each other. The app just examplifies these points.

Moreover, the documentation of the Services is elusive about the possibility of getting a service that is both started and bound. This app allows to experiment with the uncommon life cycle and understand it fully.

Finally, we also added some features to let you play with the Sticky/Non-Sticky values returned by onStartCommand. And you can also see the difference between a "normal" service and a service put in the foreground.
